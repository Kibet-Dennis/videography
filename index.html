Our Process

At Guru Creations, we believe in a structured yet flexible approach to delivering exceptional results. Our process is designed to ensure clarity, collaboration, and creativity at every step.Whether you're looking for stunning photography, engaging videography, impactful branding, or seamless streaming solutions, our process is tailored to meet your unique needs.

Consultation

We discuss your streaming needs, platform, and audience. We take time to understand your goals—whether you're building community, growing your brand, or monetizing content. We advise on the best platform and tools to deliver high-quality streams that resonate and convert.

Setup

We configure your streaming software and hardware for optimal performance. From camera and mic selection to OBS settings, lighting setups, and backup systems—we ensure professional-grade quality and reliability. You'll have confidence in every broadcast.

Live Streaming

We go live with professional quality and real-time interaction. Our team manages everything—scene switching, chat moderation, overlays—so you can focus fully on connecting with your audience and delivering great content.

Post-Stream Analysis

We provide insights on viewer engagement and performance. Get detailed analytics on viewer behavior, peak engagement times, and content performance. We recommend actionable tips to grow your audience and improve future streams.

Consultation

We plan your concept, vision, and mood board. Together, we clarify your style, audience, desired outcome, and mood. We explore locations, props, and poses that align with your brand or personal story.

Shoot Planning

We schedule, light, and storyboard. Every detail—timing, lighting direction, shot progression, wardrobe and backup plans—is mapped out so your shoot flows smoothly and minimizes stress.

Photography Session

We execute your photoshoot creatively and professionally. Our photographers guide you with posing, expressions, and movement to capture full of personality. We combine technical skill and creative vision to create stunning, authentic images.

Editing

We enhance each image to perfection. Color correction, retouching, background clean-up, and artistic styling ensure every photo aligns with your aesthetic and is ready for any use.

Delivery

We provide final photos with full rights. Receive high-resolution files for print and web, plus social formats. You’ll have full usage rights and clear organization for effortless use.

Pre‑Production

We develop concept, script, and storyboard. This is where we sharpen your message. We handle scripting, shot planning, location scouting, gear and crew prep.

Shooting

We capture cinematic footage professionally. Using quality equipment and lighting, we direct each scene with attention to framing, movement, and emotion—making sure your story comes through.

Post‑Production

We edit, grade, and design sound & graphics. Our editors refine pacing, colors, audio mixing, and motion graphics to craft an immersive and polished final video.

Final Delivery

We hand over your optimized video. We deliver in the target formats—YouTube, social media, website—along with captions, thumbnails, and usage tips to maximize impact.

Discovery

We dive into your vision, mission, and ideals. Through interviews and market research, we outline your story and audience perception, creating a brand foundation that resonates.

Concept Design

We craft logos, typography, and color palettes. You get multiple unique design directions to pick and refine—ensuring your visuals are both memorable and aligned with your identity.

Revisions

We polish designs based on your feedback. Up to 3 rounds of feedback ensure we hone your brand assets until you’re thrilled with the outcome.

Final Delivery

We deliver brand assets and guides. You’ll receive editable files (AI, PSD), record of brand colors and fonts, and usage guidelines to maintain consistency across all media.

Quotation & Agreement

We provide a detailed quote and terms. We break down costs, deliverables, and timelines clearly—so you know exactly what to expect with no surprises.

Deposit

A 50% upfront payment reserves your slot. This secures scheduling and allows us to allocate necessary resources right away—without delay.

Final Payment

Balance due at delivery. Only pay once you're fully satisfied with deliverables and we've met all agreed expectations.

Execution & Updates

We work closely with you throughout. Receive regular updates, sample reviews, and milestone check-ins—ensuring transparency and confidence every step of the way.

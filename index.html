Our Process

Consultation

We plan your photography concept with vision, location, and mood board ideas.

Shoot Planning

We arrange lighting, schedule, and shot list before your shoot day.

Editing

Post-processing and enhancement to ensure each image is flawless and on-brand.

Delivery

We deliver polished photos in print and web formats with full usage rights.

Pre-Production

Scriptwriting, scene planning, and scheduling before we roll the camera.

Shooting

Professional video shoot using 4K cameras, mics, and lighting kits.

Editing

We cut, color correct, sound mix, and apply transitions to create story flow.

Publishing

Delivered for YouTube, social media, or TV — complete with thumbnails.

Discovery

We understand your business, target audience, and values to guide the brand.

Identity Design

We design logos, color systems, and visual elements that define your brand.

Digital Setup

We build your online presence through websites, socials, and branded content.

Launch Strategy

We plan content calendars, funnels, and marketing to grow your brand visibility.

Quotation & Agreement

We begin by offering a detailed quotation outlining the cost, scope, and deliverables of the project. Once agreed, both parties sign a service agreement.

Deposit Payment

To initiate the project, a deposit of 50% is required. This confirms your booking and allows us to begin preparations and scheduling.

Service Execution

We deliver the agreed services professionally and keep you involved with progress updates, sample reviews, and timelines.

Final Payment

Upon approval of the final deliverables, the remaining balance is paid and the project is concluded with all files delivered to you.
